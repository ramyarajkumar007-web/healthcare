🍭 DIABETES

Introduction

Diabetes is a disease where the body cannot control sugar levels properly.

What is Diabetes?

It happens when insulin is not produced or not used properly.

Types

Type 1, Type 2, and Gestational diabetes.

Causes

Unhealthy food, lack of exercise, stress, and genetics.

Symptoms

Thirst, tiredness, blurred vision, frequent urination.

Effects

Damage to heart, eyes, kidneys, and nerves.

Prevention

Healthy diet, exercise, proper sleep.

More Information

Diabetes is increasing due to lifestyle changes.
