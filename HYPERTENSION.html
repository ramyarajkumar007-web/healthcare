😵 HYPERTENSION

Introduction

Hypertension is high blood pressure.

Causes

Salt, stress, lack of exercise.

Symptoms

Headache, dizziness, tiredness.

Effects

Heart problems, stroke.

Prevention

Exercise, healthy diet.

More Information

It is a silent disease.

Extra Details

Regular monitoring is important.

Further Explanation

Reducing salt helps control it.

More Information

Stress increases blood pressure.

Extra Details

Relaxation helps control it.

Further Explanation

Healthy lifestyle prevents it.

Conclusion

Control is possible with care.
